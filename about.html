About me

Hi, my name is Anna! I will tell you a little about myself. I am 33 years old. My profession is graphic designer.

I have been drawing since childhood. And from the age of 16, I began to make pictures not with a brush or pencil, but with a camera.

Completed a photography course in 2012.

About Anna Kovalenko photography

What is Anna Kovalenko photography? This is on-location photography, so I travel to the photo shoot location that you choose. Of course, I will help you with practical advice on where it is best to shoot. And how best to prepare for a certain type of shooting. After you leave me your contact information, I will contact you within 24 hours.

Since I am a beginner photographer in Ireland, at the moment I do not have my own photo studio. But I have all the necessary equipment and knowledge to take great pictures.
